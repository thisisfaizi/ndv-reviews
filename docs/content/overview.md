# NDV Reviews

**NDV Reviews** is a reliable, self-hosted product reviews plugin for **WooCommerce**, built by Nowdigiverse. It is designed as a faster, privacy-first alternative to ReviewX and WiserReview.

## The one-line pitch

The reliable, self-hosted WooCommerce reviews plugin — multi-criteria ratings, photo/video reviews, working reminders, AI summaries, and Q&A — with no forced SaaS account.

## What makes it different

- **Self-hosted, no account required.** The free plugin works fully with zero external calls. All review data stays in the store's own database.
- **No metering.** Unlimited reviews, requests, and storage — because it runs on the merchant's server.
- **Reliable review reminders.** Reminder emails run on a background queue (Action Scheduler) with a visible log, retry, and test-send — fixing the single most common complaint about competitors.
- **Honest free tier.** Full review moderation, including editing, is free.
- **You own your data.** Stronger GDPR story — review data never leaves the store.

## Two-plugin architecture

- `ndv-reviews` {badge:free:FREE} — published on WordPress.org. Genuinely useful on its own.
- `ndv-reviews-pro` {badge:pro:PRO} — a separate add-on for automation, scale, AI, and extra channels. Boots on the `ndv-reviews/loaded` hook and never edits free files.
